🔒 Privacy & Telemetry

📊 GDPR-Compliant Data Collection

Y Social respects your privacy and is fully compliant with GDPR regulations. We collect minimal, anonymized telemetry data to improve the platform. Here’s exactly what we track:

What We Collect

📈 Usage Analytics
  • # Experiments/Populations
  • # Agents/Pages
  • Recommendation system used
  • Simulation duration
  • Preferred LLM model
💾 Installation Data
  • Installation/Update date
  • YSocial version
  • Operating system and locale
🐛 Error Logs
  • Error types and frequency
  • Stack traces (anonymized)

What We DON’T Collect

✅ Your Privacy is Protected
  • ❌ No personally identifiable information (PII)
  • ❌ No IP addresses or location data
  • ❌ No simulation content or user-generated data
  • ❌ No email addresses or contact information
  • ❌ No passwords or authentication credentials
  • ❌ No tracking of individual users across sessions

Your Rights & Control

🎛️ Opt-Out Anytime: You have full control over telemetry data collection. To disable just set the appropriate toggle in the admin panel.

🔐 Data Security: All telemetry data is:

  • Anonymized and aggregated to protect your identity
  • Stored securely with access controls
  • Used solely for improving Y Social
  • Never shared with third parties

Why We Collect Data

We use this information to:

  • 🐛 Identify and fix bugs faster
  • 📊 Understand which features are most valuable
  • ⚡ Improve performance and reliability
  • 🎯 Prioritize development efforts
  • 🔒 Enhance security

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and respect for user privacy. This telemetry helps us build a better product without compromising your privacy. Check with your own eyes what we collect in the service dedicated repository (yTelemetry) and make an informed decision.
